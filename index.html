I Thought of That

A love song for Christine Goodwin

Written with love for my wife, Christine Goodwin. This song is about the small things that matter most — showing up, loving fully, staying close through hard times, and building a life together one day at a time.

About

I Thought of That is a song about love done right. It is not about regret — it is about gratitude, commitment, and the kind of love that lasts.

This page was made as a dedication to Christine Goodwin and as a simple GitHub Pages site that can be shared, saved, and remembered.
